How it works

01

Score

An autonomous engine reads price action, fundamentals, catalysts and risk for thousands of stocks, around the clock, and grades each into a conviction band.

02

Measure

Every score is frozen the moment it's made. Seven and thirty days later, the machine checks what the price actually did against the S&P 500, adjusted for how risky the stock was.

03

Publish

The full ledger, hits, misses, and the current verdict, is regenerated nightly and published here. If the edge ever fades, this page will say so before we do.
